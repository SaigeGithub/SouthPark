All about SouthPark Guide app

Q1:What does the app do?
The idea of this app it to create a app for all the fans of southpark, which contain all the information about it. The users can check to see the brief intro of the animation,or check the chart to find his favourite character and get to know the detail information of him/her It is like a wikipedia just for southpark. Anyway,the users can also enjoy the gallery of the funny pic in southpark.

Q2:What are the features of it?
This web app will change the appearance to suit browser,so it is convenient for mobile phone users.Compared to an actual app in ios/Android,it do not need to install,you can use it anyplace in any machine.
Besides that, the app is easy to use, it has a straight forward operation interface,you just need to click the navigation bars and everything is done.

Q3:How does it fill in the need of market place?
There are southpark fans all around the world but there is not a one web designed for phone about the informations of southpark. I have see some applications designed for games and animation, for example the ACNL Guide in ios platform(Animal Crossing Guide), and it is really helpful! And my app is kind of similar to it, which means it has commercial value in some way
